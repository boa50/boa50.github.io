How much money do we need to be happy?

This project was created to verify the influence of money on our happiness levels.
The most important insight derived from the analysis is that there isn't a linear relationship between money and happiness and many other factors could interfere with this relationship.
Some analyses try to infer a direct correlation between money and happiness, but we could see that these variables isolated could not show a good representation of causation.
This analysis was important to show people that trying to get more money will not always help them to have a better life.

Technologies used:

It was created using datasets from many sources and using R language.
